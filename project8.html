Pain itself is something to be loved and pursued, but sometimes circumstances occur in which toil and pain can bring great pleasure. Nor again is there anyone who loves or pursues or desires to obtain pain simply because it is pain, but occasionally circumstances occur in which toil and pain can bring about some great pleasure. To take a trivial example, who among us ever undertakes laborious physical exercise except to obtain some advantage from it?
